About us: The website has been created for the purpose of providing help and for all people with psychological problems, giving tips, and recommending professionals in the area who can help in the best way.

Taking care of your Mental Health and making it healthy is our goal. Therefore, we provide contacts of experts and related institutions to self-care and emotional health.

Here you will find articles and guides explaining how you can take special care of yourself and ensure your stability.

Therapy Types: Learn about different types of therapy, such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), psychodynamic therapy, and more.

Self-care Tips: Find practical tips on how to take care of your mental and emotional well-being every day.

Support Groups: Discover various support groups for different mental health conditions where you can connect with others.

Mindfulness Practices: Learn about mindfulness and meditation practices to help manage stress and improve mental clarity.

Emergency Contacts: Quick access to emergency hotlines and resources for immediate mental health assistance.

Recommended Books: A selection of books that can guide you through self-care, therapy, and understanding mental health better:

  • The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle
  • Feeling Good: The New Mood Therapy by David D. Burns
  • Man's Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl
  • Self-Compassion by Kristin Neff
  • The Body Keeps the Score by Bessel van der Kolk
